MetadataCleaner for macOS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Short version: MetadataCleaner does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything happens locally on your Mac. We have no servers, no analytics, no tracking.

🔒 What We Collect

MetadataCleaner collects no personal information.

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • The files you clean or their contents
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Location data

📂 File Processing

All file processing happens 100% locally on your Mac. Your files are never uploaded to any server or transmitted over the internet.

MetadataCleaner only reads and modifies files that you explicitly select or drop into the application. The app does not access any files without your direct action.

🗓 Trial Period Tracking

To manage the 90-day free trial, MetadataCleaner stores a single date — your first launch date — in your Mac's Keychain.

This information:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to anyone except the app itself
  • Contains no personal information — only a date

A daily file counter is stored in local app preferences (UserDefaults) and resets automatically each day.

💳 Purchases

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple's App Store. MetadataCleaner never processes or stores payment information.

When you purchase Pro, Apple sends a confirmation to the app. We store only a boolean flag (purchased: yes/no) in your local app preferences. No payment details are ever accessible to us.

For questions about payments, refunds, or purchase history, please contact Apple Support at support.apple.com.

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

MetadataCleaner is a native macOS application. It does not use cookies, web beacons, pixels, or any other tracking technologies.

👶 Children's Privacy

MetadataCleaner does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no features directed at children.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
